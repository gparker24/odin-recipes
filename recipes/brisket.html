Smoked Brisket

Home

Description

This is a recipe for smoked brisket. These simple steps are easy to perform, and from there all it takes is patience as it can take many hours to finish cooking. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • wood chips
  • ¼ cup paprika
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • ¼ cup ground cumin
  • ¼ cup cayenne pepper
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup chili powder
  • ¼ cup garlic powder
  • ¼ cup onion powder
  • ¼ cup kosher salt
  • ¼ cup freshly cracked black pepper
  • 10 pounds beef brisket, or more to taste

Steps

  1. Gather Ingredients.
  2. Soak wood chips overnight.
  3. trim any hard fat off the surface of the brisket and reduce fat layer to approximately 1/4 inch.
  4. Mix spices and coat brisket, then refriderate 24 hours.
  5. Preheat smoker 225 degrees F. Drain wood chips and place in smoker.
  6. Smoke brisket in the smoker until the internal temp reaches 165 degrees F. Wrap tightly with foil or butcher paper and return to smoker.
  7. Continue cooking until tender, likely around 200 degrees F.
  8. Let the brisket rest for 20 minutes in order to prevent it from continuing to cook past tender.
  9. Slice the brisket into 1/4 inch slices across the grain of the muscle fibers and serve.
